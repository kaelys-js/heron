Heron -- Privacy Policy

Heron is local-first. Your data stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Heron has no analytics, no tracking, and no Heron-operated servers. Your CV, applications, reports, and email triage live in a local database on your own device or self-hosted instance.

Your AI keys

AI features are bring-your-own-key. When you add an Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google key, requests go directly from your device/instance to that provider under their privacy policy. Heron never sees or proxies those requests.

Job postings & email

Postings you paste and recruiter emails you connect are processed locally to draft tailored documents and track status. They are not transmitted to us.
