Heron is a personal job-search productivity tool.

• Tailor CVs and cover letters per role with on-device LLMs (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google — use your own API key)
• Scan 11+ job portals: LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Ashby, Lever, Workday, SmartRecruiters, Workable, Indeed, Wellfound, Ladders, plus generic ATS support
• Track every application in a local SQLite database — never leaves the device
• Prep interviews with mock-question + STAR-story drills, plus per-company research dossiers
• Receive follow-up reminders at the optimal cadence (3-day, 7-day, 14-day windows)
• Multi-platform: iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Mac, Windows, Linux, web

Heron is open-source (MIT) and self-hostable. The dashboard runs on your Mac, your phone connects via secure local network or Tailscale, and your data stays on YOUR machines.

Built with Capacitor + SvelteKit + Better Auth (passkey-first).

Source: https://github.com/kaelys-js/heron
